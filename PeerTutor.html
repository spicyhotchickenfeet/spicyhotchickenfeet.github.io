ACT - Climate Change Club

The aim of Peer Tutor Club is to give 11th and 12th grade students an opportunity to openly share their knowledge with students from 9th and 10th grades. They answer in detail to questions related to current topics covered during classes, help with homework problems, and provide guidance to projects. For all students this is a great opportunity to develop communication and critical thinking skills. The club gives a possibility to develop the basics of teaching principals.

Grades: 9,10,11,12
