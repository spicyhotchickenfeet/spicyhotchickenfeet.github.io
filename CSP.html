Cameroon Service Project

The Cameroon Service Project is a student-led group that organises events and activities through the school year to support a village in Cameroon with water access and education. Each year we design and sell T-shirts and students are encouraged to create, manage, and complete a variety of small projects that not only benefit the project but also develop their skills. Examples have included creating and selling photographic calendars, organising Primary School Movie Night and also HS Sports tournaments. Everyone is welcome to join us and be part of the team.
