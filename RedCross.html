International Red Cross Group

The International Red Cross Group aims to provide students with basic First Aid skills, and an understanding of how they can best respond to emergency situations of various types. They also learn about the work of the Red Cross, both in Norway and overseas, and arrange events to raise awareness and funds for the organisation.
